The Krusty Krab

~"The Krabby Patty is what ties us all together! Without it, there will be a complete breakdown of social order!"

The Krusty Krab is a fast food restaurant in the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants. In the television show the restaurant is famous for its signature burger, the Krabby Patty, the formula to which is a closely guarded trade secret.

The restaurant was founded by Eugene H. Krabs (often referred to as Mr. Krabs), who is also the owner and manager.


The Krusty Krab's menu, the Galley Grub, consists mostly of fast-food items, such as burgers and sodas. Its signature burgers, the Krabby Patty, is comically beloved by the citizens of Bikini Bottom.
